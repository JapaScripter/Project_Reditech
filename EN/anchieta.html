VOLKSWAGEN FACTORY (Buffer) - ANCHIETA SP

Reditech programmed the buffer for engines and suspension mounting plates for the Polo, Virtus and Saveiro in ALA 14 department – ‘combining engine and body’.

The scope of supply included:

  • Programming of the tracking system and interface with the Volkswagen MES-system;
  • PLC programming;
  • Development of operating logic and HMI for the new models.


The system consists of a buffer of roll tables which, in addition to storing the assembly boards, the system also manages and organizes the order and sequence of supply of boards to the line. According to the chassis list supplied by Volkswagen's central system, each car model has its own specific plate.
