  • A multimedia filter, an activated carbon filter and a reverse osmosis system. Its basic operation consists of pumping the raw water from the well and storing this water in the 20 m³ tank via a multimedia filter made up of anthracite, sand, garnet and pebble filter beds.


In the Reverse Osmosis System's feed line, the presence of chlorine, pH and turbidity are measured. If necessary, NaOH is dosed to adjust the pH and then the anti-fouling agent is dosed. The permeate will be sent to the Permeate Water Tank and the waste generated will be sent to the Effluent Tank.
