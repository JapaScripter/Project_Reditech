PERFETTI VAN MELLE FACTORY - VINHEDO SP

At the Perfetti-Van Melle factory, Reditech developed control software for the Effluent Treatment Plant, responsible for treating the water discharged from the production area during production process throughout the year. The scope of supply included:

  • Electrical design in Eplan;


  • Assembly of the automation and MCC panels;


  • Supply of operating station and engineering licenses;


  • Development of PLC logic and the operating screens.


The system consists of an anaerobic reactor, a wastewater conditioning tank, a new activated sludge system using MBR (Membrane Bioreactor) technology, as well as systems for interconnection and burning the biogas produced.

CASE

Its basic operation consists of collecting the effluent from the production area, which will be pre-treated, equalized and treated anaerobically in the EGSB reactor (expanded granular sludge bed) and by the aerobic system consisting of:

  • Raw effluent collection pumps;


  • Pre-treatment: sieving, removal of grease, sand and solids, equalization tanks;


  • Buffer Tank and the DAF (dissolved air flotation);


  • Aerobic plant (activated sludge process + MBR); - Booster pumps and transfer and CIP tanks.
