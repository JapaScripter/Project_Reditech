MERCEDES-BENZ SPOT REPAIR

This consists of a system that performs a water penetration test, where the vehicle passes through two 7-minute stages of water jets using a conveyor belt.

At the end of this process, the vehicle passes through a ventilated dryer so that in the next stage, the operator can check the vehicle body for leaks.

Reditech developed the software for the HMI and the PLC that controls the pumps, conveyor belt and Interlock with the dryer, which already had its own control.

Shower Test

This consists of a system with a burner and a water spray that are combined to create the perfect temperature and humidity for the paint to dry evenly.

The entire cycle can be adjusted according to the specifications of the model being produced.

Reditech developed the software for the HMI which assists the operation process, as there is a cooling down time for the cabin securing that the operator can safely access the cabin, the PLC control logic adjusts this time automatically according to the model.
