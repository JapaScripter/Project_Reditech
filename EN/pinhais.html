VOLKSWAGEN FACTORY (Frames) - SÃO JOSÉ DOS PINHAIS PR

Reditech carried out using the ‘VASS standard’ all programming of the new conveyor line in the Volkswagen Curitiba ‘frame’ departent for the production of the T-Cross. The scope of supply included:

  • PLC programming;


  • Development of operating logic and HMI for the new conveyors.


The project consisted of the programming and start-up of 6 conveyor lines starting from the handling of the supply of skids to the final assembly of the car, totaling more than 1000 meters of conveyors.
